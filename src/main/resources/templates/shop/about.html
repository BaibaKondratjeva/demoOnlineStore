We are CREATIVE GUNS

We believe in weapons! You ask why? It's quite simple and simultaneously not really. People tend to say that the world needs to be looked at more broadly, but we believe that we need to focus sharply, directly, and concretely on our goals. And what is more direct, if not through the barrel of the weapon. Shooting is a way to nurture patience, character, concentration, and attention. Shooting frees the mind from unnecessary thoughts. It makes you try. Shooting teaches that only precisely fired bullets reach the target, but the ability to not surrender in combination with willpower will help to get closer and closer. Yes, sometimes weapons give people a sense of power, but it should be more about power and control over themselves, their emotions, and character. Without trust, cooperation is not possible. And we trust our customers deeply. As an American journalist, novelist, essayist, and poet, Christopher Morely once said, "Big shots are just little shots who keep shooting." So - blow a kiss, fire a gun!
